## Problem

The project investigated how key music attributes such as danceability and energy influence track popularity, using a real-world Spotify dataset.

## Data

- Real Spotify track-level feature dataset
- Features included danceability, energy, and other audio attributes
- Popularity score used as the core target variable for analysis

## Approach

- Performed clustering with Python and scikit-learn (K-Means) to identify potential genre and style patterns
- Built correlation matrices to detect key factors associated with popularity
- Designed and implemented 3 interactive Tableau dashboards for drill-down analysis and storytelling

## Outcome & Impact

- Identified meaningful feature groupings and factor relationships that explain popularity trends
- Improved interpretability of insights through interactive visual analytics
- Delivered a reusable analytics workflow for feature-based music market exploration
