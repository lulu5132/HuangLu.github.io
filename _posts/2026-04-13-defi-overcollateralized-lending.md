## Problem

The project aimed to build a complete DeFi lending protocol on Ethereum Sepolia testnet with secure collateral, liquidation, and interest mechanisms.

## Data

- On-chain transaction and contract-state data from Sepolia testnet
- User interaction and collateral behavior observed through dApp workflows

## Approach

- Implemented core smart contract logic in Solidity for collateralization, liquidation, and interest accrual
- Used Hardhat for contract development, testing, and deployment
- Integrated contracts with a front-end application for end-to-end lending interactions

## Outcome & Impact

- Successfully deployed and validated a functional overcollateralized lending workflow on testnet
- Demonstrated full protocol flow from collateral deposit to borrowing and liquidation handling
- Produced a complete project package including source code, demo video, and live app
